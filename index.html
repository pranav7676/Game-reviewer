Gamiee Reverie

Need for Speed: Most Wanted

EA Black Box

Need for Speed: Most Wanted is one of the most iconic entries in the Need for Speed franchise and is often considered one of the best racing games of its time.

Elden Ring

FromSoftware

Elden Ring is an open-world action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware. It has received immense critical acclaim for its intricate world-building and challenging gameplay.

Tomb Raider

Crystal Dynamics

Tomb Raider follows the adventures of Lara Croft in her quest to uncover ancient secrets. Known for its gripping story and adventurous gameplay, it's a staple in the action-adventure genre.

Grand Theft Auto V

Rockstar Games

Grand Theft Auto V is an open-world action-adventure game. Set in the fictional city of Los Santos, it offers an expansive world filled with missions, heists, and endless activities.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

CD Projekt Red

The Witcher 3 is an action role-playing game that follows Geralt of Rivia as he searches for his adopted daughter. The game is praised for its deep story, open world, and engaging gameplay.

Red Dead Redemption 2

Rockstar Games

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a Western-themed action-adventure game. Set in 1899, it follows Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang as they deal with the changing world and law enforcement.

Cyberpunk 2077

CD Projekt Red

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world RPG set in the dystopian Night City. Players control V, a mercenary with a customizable past, on a quest for immortality.

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II

Infinity Ward

Modern Warfare II is a first-person shooter game and the sequel to 2019's Modern Warfare. It offers thrilling combat experiences and an engaging single-player campaign.

Assassin's Creed Odyssey

Ubisoft

Assassin's Creed Odyssey is an action RPG set in Ancient Greece. It features a vast open world with deep narrative choices, combat, and exploration.

Fortnite

Epic Games

Fortnite is a free-to-play battle royale game where players compete to be the last one standing. Its constantly evolving world and events keep players coming back.

League of Legends

Riot Games

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) where players control champions and work together to destroy the enemy's Nexus. It's known for its competitive nature and strategy.

Overwatch 2

Blizzard Entertainment

Overwatch 2 is a team-based first-person shooter that focuses on objective-based gameplay. Players select from various heroes with unique abilities to complete objectives.

FIFA 23

EA Sports

FIFA 23 is the latest entry in the FIFA football series. It features realistic graphics, updated teams, and a variety of gameplay modes, including Ultimate Team.

Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Nintendo

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a life simulation game where players build their own island paradise. It's a relaxing experience with crafting, fishing, and villager interactions.

Minecraft

Mojang

Minecraft is a sandbox game where players build and explore a blocky world. It's known for its creative mode, survival mechanics, and vast community of creators.

Super Mario Odyssey

Nintendo

Super Mario Odyssey is a 3D platformer where Mario embarks on a global adventure to rescue Princess Peach. Its innovative mechanics and open-world exploration make it a standout title.

Hades

Supergiant Games

Hades is a rogue-like action game where players take on the role of Zagreus, the son of Hades, as he attempts to escape the underworld. Its fast-paced combat and deep narrative are highly praised.

Rocket League

Psyonix

Rocket League is a unique sports game that combines soccer with rocket-powered cars. It's fast-paced, competitive, and offers multiplayer fun for players of all ages.

Halo Infinite

343 Industries

Halo Infinite is a first-person shooter that continues the story of Master Chief. With a new open-world design, it delivers a fresh take on the iconic franchise.

Spiderman: Miles Morales

Insomniac Games

Spider-Man: Miles Morales is an action-packed superhero game where players control Miles Morales as he protects New York City. It features fluid combat and a compelling story.

Add Game

Hitman 3

IO Interactive

Hitman 3 is the final chapter in the World of Assassination trilogy. It offers stealth-based gameplay where players control Agent 47 as he completes various assassination missions around the world.

Monster Hunter: World

Capcom

Monster Hunter: World is an action RPG where players hunt giant monsters in an expansive open world. It emphasizes cooperation in multiplayer mode and offers various weapons and strategies.

Dead by Daylight

Behaviour Interactive

Dead by Daylight is an asymmetric survival horror game where one player takes on the role of a killer and the others must work together to escape. The game features iconic horror movie villains.

Dark Souls III

FromSoftware

Dark Souls III is an action RPG known for its punishing difficulty and intricate world design. It follows the story of the Ashen One as they battle through a dying world to defeat powerful enemies.

Battlefield V

EA DICE

Battlefield V is a first-person shooter that immerses players in World War II. The game features large-scale multiplayer battles, destructible environments, and a variety of vehicles.

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Nintendo

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is an open-world action-adventure game. Players control Link as he explores Hyrule, solves puzzles, and battles enemies to defeat Ganon and save Princess Zelda.

Subnautica

Unknown Worlds Entertainment

Subnautica is an open-world survival game set in the depths of an alien ocean. Players must gather resources, explore, and uncover secrets while surviving underwater.

Sea of Thieves

Rare

Sea of Thieves is a multiplayer action-adventure game where players sail the seas as pirates, searching for treasure, battling enemies, and completing quests in a shared world.

Stardew Valley

ConcernedApe

Stardew Valley is a farming simulation game where players inherit a rundown farm and must restore it while building relationships with the town's inhabitants. It's a relaxing and rewarding experience.

Final Fantasy VII Remake

Square Enix

Final Fantasy VII Remake reimagines the classic RPG with updated graphics and real-time combat. It tells the story of Cloud Strife and his quest to stop the megacorporation Shinra from exploiting the planet.

Persona 5 Royal

Atlus

Persona 5 Royal is a turn-based RPG that follows a group of high school students with the ability to enter the Metaverse and battle corrupted adults. The game is known for its deep story and social simulation.

Gears 5

The Coalition

Gears 5 is a third-person shooter that follows Kait Diaz as she battles against the Swarm. The game features a cinematic story and cooperative multiplayer modes.

Dragon Age: Inquisition

BioWare

Dragon Age: Inquisition is an action RPG set in the fantasy world of Thedas. Players control the Inquisitor and gather a team to combat the forces of darkness threatening the world.

DOOM Eternal

id Software

DOOM Eternal is a fast-paced first-person shooter that follows the Doom Slayer as he battles demons across hellish landscapes. The game features brutal combat and an adrenaline-pumping soundtrack.

Watch Dogs: Legion

Ubisoft

Watch Dogs: Legion is an open-world action game set in a dystopian London. Players can recruit any NPC to join their cause, each with unique abilities and skills.

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Bethesda

Skyrim is an open-world RPG set in the Nordic region of Tamriel. Players take on the role of the Dragonborn and explore a world filled with dragons, magic, and quests.

Spelunky 2

Mossmouth

Spelunky 2 is a challenging roguelike platformer where players explore procedurally generated caves. The game requires skillful jumping and problem-solving to survive its brutal traps and enemies.

Hollow Knight

Team Cherry

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania action-adventure game set in a mysterious underground kingdom. It features tight platforming, difficult bosses, and an atmospheric world to explore.

Bloodborne

FromSoftware

Bloodborne is an action RPG set in a gothic, nightmare-infested world. Players control a hunter as they battle terrifying creatures and uncover the mysteries behind the cursed city of Yharnam.

Resident Evil Village

Capcom

Resident Evil Village is the eighth entry in the survival horror series. It follows Ethan Winters as he explores a mysterious village to rescue his daughter, facing horrifying monsters along the way.
